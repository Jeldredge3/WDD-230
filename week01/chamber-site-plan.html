Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Sandy City Chamber

The city of Sandy in Utah is often called Sandy City, which sounded well with Chamber added at the end of it. The website URL will be www.sandycitychamber.us.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Sandy City Chamber website is to create a community and networking opportunities for businesses. Local businesses of Sandy, UT, big and small, will be able to connect and communicate together using the resources provided by the website.

Another purpose of the website is to inform members and visitors of local events and activities regarding Sandy, UT. There will be spotlight businesses which will be featured on the website which will help bring exposure to participating companies.

Scenarios

What businesses are participating in the Sandy City Chamber?

Should my business join the Chamber of Commerce in the area?

What upcoming events can my business participate in?

How can I contact and learn more about local businesses?

This accent color is a medium brown which will may not be used much, but will appear well on dark backgrounds.
