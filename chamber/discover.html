Dimpledell Regional Park

This park is a great spot for recreational activities and offers something for everyone. There are trails for hiking, mountain biking, and horseback riding. It's a wonderful area to relax during the summer!

Sandy Amphitheater

This outdoor venue features a great view of the Wasatch Mountains and offers a variety of events throughout the year. The amphitheater hosts concerts, theatrical performances, and local festivals.

Rio Tinto Stadium

The Rio Tinto Stadium is a premier soccer venue home to the Major League Soccer team, Real Salt Lake, along with the Women's Soccer League's Utah Royals FC. The stadium was opened in 2008 and offers seating for over 20,000 fans.

The Shops at South Town

This shopping center is home to many unique shops and resturaunts. The area also includes an enclosed regional mall containing many big name brand stores. There are also local venues for entertainment, including a movie theater, bowling ally, and a miniture golf course.

Mountain America Expo Center

This event center offers a wide variety conferences, events and expos throughout each year. The event center has over 1,000 parking spaces and features a total of 243,000 square feet in meeting rooms.

Demographic Info for Sandy, Utah

This rapidly growing city in the Salt Lake Valley of Utah has an estimated population over 99,000 as of 2023. Sandy, Utah has a diverse and multi-cultural community which is welcoming to all.
